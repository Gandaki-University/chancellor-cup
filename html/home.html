About Us

Gandaki University

By 2043, Gandaki University hopes to have produced all of the highly skilled workers needed to create a thriving province and contented citizens. The university was founded in 2019. Students are given the knowledge and skills they need at the university to meet the challenges of the twenty-first century. The institution wants to become a hub of academic and research excellence, benefiting the country by developing highly qualified labor in a variety of fields.
